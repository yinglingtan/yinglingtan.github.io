Before I Met You

Conceived as an experiment in profiling and text analysis, Before I Met You is a participatory digital art piece driven by the notion of true empathy. If you have ever felt that someone does not understand how you feel, we would like you to examine the origin of the gap. Where does it come from? How can we explore ways to bridge this disconnection? The first step towards empathy is openness. Here, we invite you to write an earnest letter of your personal feelings and thoughts to your imaginary friend. Dive deep into what truly matters to you.


Before I Met You will be exhibited at ArtScience Museum as part of Betwixt Festival: Art & Bytes

Initial Concept: Empathy Matching Algorithm

Empathy is typically defined as feeling what another person is feeling. When we ask people: “How do you know if a person understands how you feel?” The reply we often get is: “I know it because he/she has gone through similar experience as me.” I.e. empathy is feeling what another person is feeling if both of you share similar stories. Therefore, we ponder about how it feels when two complete strangers with the same stories meet?
