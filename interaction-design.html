User Persona

Chat Planner is perfect for Mark, a tech-savy young adult who loves traveling with friends. It is designed for anyone to conveniently plan a trip with your travel buddy while chatting on the go.



Use Case

Emma wants to plan a trip with Mark and texts him on Facebook Messenger. Mark clicks on the underlined keywords and Chat Planner gets activated. It first asks Mark for the destination and point of origin.



Chat Planner recommeds tour packages and flights for Mark's travel destination with real-time prices and information powered by Sabre Travel APIs



Once Mark and Emma have completed all the bookings, Chat Planner generates an organized travel plan for him to proceed to payment.



Unique Value Proposition

Chat Planner offers a supreme user experience by allowing Mark to have a full and enriching conversation with another human, Emma, while the bot works in the background and present possibilities into the conversation seamlessly.







The Technology

Built with Facebook Messenger and wit.ai bot engine, Chat Planner uses artifical intelligence to identify and translate Mark's conversations into a travel plan.
