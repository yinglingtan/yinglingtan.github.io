XFERS CHECKOUT FLOW REDESIGN


The checkout flow is part of the Xfers Redirect payment product. A redirect payments solution meant that buyers would place their orders on the merchants’ website, and be redirected to Xfers checkout flow to complete the payment.

Note: This redesign project is in the pipeline and has not been launched yet.

Checkout Flowchart


Phase 1 - SHOP


Buyers shop on merchant’s site and get redirected to Xfers to complete payment.
Here's an example from Airpaz, our merchant.

Phase 2 - SIGN IN AS GUEST


Buyers create a guest account by going through a phone number verification.


Phase 2 - SIGN IN WITH XFERS ACCOUNT


Buyers login to their account with their email and password and their balance will be displayed.


Phase 3 - DETAILS


The fields are disclosed progressively to prevent buyers from feeling overwhelmed.

Phase 4 - PAYMENT & CONFIRMATION





Deduct from Xfers account when there is sufficient balance.

Top up Xfers Account when there is insufficient balance.

Pay via credit card and save card for future transactions.

Conclusion


Ultimately, Xfers checkout offer 3 forms of payment methods, while the rest are building blocks to create a complete checkout flow.
