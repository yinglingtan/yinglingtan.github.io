Before I Met You

Conceived as an experiment in profiling and text analysis, Before I Met You is a participatory digital art piece driven by the notion of true empathy.If you have ever felt that someone does not understand how you feel, we would like you to examine the origin of the gap. Where does it come from? How can we explore ways to bridge this disconnection?

This project will be exhibited at ArtScience Museum as part of Betwixt Festival: Art & Bytes
