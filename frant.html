Frant x Startup Weekend SG

Frant is a voice messaging app that makes ranting fun with friends! This mobile app was born in Startup Weekend SG 2015, and was voted as the top 5 startup idea among 20 over participating teams.

Identifying the Problem

More than 20,000 tertiary students in Singapore are facing stress everyday and they need an avenue to rant. However, 3/5 students would rather suppress their negativity than getting judged by their friends. Suppressing stress overtime can increase the chances of students falling into depression.

Mapping a User Journey

My team's vision is to ensure that Frant removes the social stigma of ranting by transforming stress into fun! Using the voice distortion tool, students can create hilarious clips of their rant and share it with friends. Ranting instantly becomes the catalyst for laughter.

Sketching

Fun and simplicity are the main principles of this app. Using the user journey as a guide, I sketched a few options for considerations. My team and I explored different ways users play with the voice distortion tool.

Creating Wireframes

Analyzing the sketches led us to decide on large icons as buttons to make each voice distortion feature stand out on its own. Most importantly, users feel that they have a gallery of voice distortions to play with and create a unique twist to their rant.

Final Result

My team presented this as our final product at Startup Weekend SG and cracked up the whole room. We went for a funky user interface with bright colors and playful icons that would appeal to the student communities.
